About Us

Vaksācija Zolītūde & Imanta

Marina.BeautyRoom is Riga's proudly women-owned waxing studio, serving the Zolītūde and Imanta neighbourhoods from our location at Ruses iela 6-1. With a perfect 5.0 star rating and 28 Google reviews, our clients trust us for professional, caring treatments.
