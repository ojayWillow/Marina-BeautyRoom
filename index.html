Our Story

Why Terezas Waxing?

Terezas Waxing & Body Studio is Riga's trusted destination for professional hair removal, located in the city centre at Dzirnavu iela 31. We welcome women, men and pregnant clients in a clean, comfortable and private environment.

Our certified therapists use only premium wax — gentle enough for sensitive skin — and every treatment is tailored to your individual needs. Whether it's your very first wax or you're a regular, you're in expert hands.
